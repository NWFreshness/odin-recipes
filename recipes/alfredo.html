Chicken Fettuccini Alfredo

The best pasta dish around!

Mom’s creamy Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo has been a family favorite for years. Classic Chicken Alfredo is a feel good comfort food – so creamy but light!

Ingredients
  • 2lbs Chicken
  • 3/4lbs fettuccine pasta
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 1/2 cups cream
  • 4 cups parmesean cheese
  • 1 cup butter
Steps
  1. Cook chicken in cast iron
  2. Boil fettuccini until tender
  3. Mix butter, cream, and cheese and bring to a boil
  4. Combine fettuccine, alfredo sauce, and chicken
